## Full Description

**CitiMovers Driver** is the official partner app for professional delivery drivers in the Philippines. Join our growing network of verified drivers and earn money by delivering packages, moving items, and providing reliable transport services to customers across the country.

### 🚗 Why Drive with CitiMovers?

**Flexible Schedule**
- Be your own boss - work when you want
- Go online to receive delivery requests
- Go offline when you need a break
- No minimum hours required

**Competitive Earnings**
- Earn up to 80% of delivery fare
- Track your earnings in real-time
- View detailed earnings breakdown
- Get paid weekly or on-demand

**Easy-to-Use App**
- Simple delivery request acceptance
- Step-by-step delivery guidance
- Photo verification at key stages
- Real-time GPS navigation

**Reliable Support**
- 24/7 driver support available
- Clear delivery instructions
- In-app chat with customers
- Direct customer calling

### 📱 Key Features

**Delivery Management**
- Receive delivery requests when online
- Accept or reject requests based on your preference
- View all delivery details before accepting
- Track active, completed, and cancelled deliveries
- Share delivery details with others

**Delivery Progress Tracking**
- Step-by-step delivery guidance:
  - Heading to pickup location
  - Arrived at pickup
  - Start loading
  - Finish loading
  - Heading to delivery location
  - Arrived at delivery
  - Start unloading
  - Finish unloading
  - Delivery completed

**Photo Verification**
- Take photos at key delivery stages
- Confirm pickup with photo
- Confirm delivery with photo
- Verify receiver's ID
- Capture receiver's signature

**Earnings Dashboard**
- View your wallet balance in real-time
- Track total deliveries completed
- Monitor your average rating
- View earnings by period (Today, This Week, This Month, All Time)
- Visual earnings charts (weekly and monthly)
- Detailed transaction history
- Load your wallet easily

**Profile & Vehicle Management**
- Manage your personal information
- Update vehicle details (type, model, color, plate number)
- Upload vehicle photo
- Manage up to 2 helpers/crew members
- Track helper documents

**Document Management**
Upload and manage required documents:
- Driver's License
- Vehicle Registration (OR/CR)
- LTFRB Permit
- Insurance
- NBI Clearance
- Drug Test
- Police Clearance
- Fit to Work Certificate
- Resume/Biodata
- Valid ID
- Unit Photo (front with plate visible)
- Helper documents (for up to 2 helpers)

**Settings & Preferences**
- Toggle online/offline status
- Customize notification preferences
- Enable/disable sound effects and vibration
- Configure location services
- Set language preference
- Choose theme (Light/Dark)
- Auto-accept deliveries option

**Communication**
- Real-time chat with customers
- Direct call to customers
- Receive delivery notifications
- Get important updates from CitiMovers

### 💰 How Earnings Work

**Earnings Distribution**
- **Operator (You):** 80% of delivery fare
- **Admin:** 18% of delivery fare
- **BIR (Tax):** 2% of delivery fare

**Payment Options**
- Weekly payouts
- On-demand wallet withdrawal
- Secure payment processing
- Transparent transaction history

### 🌟 How It Works

1. **Sign Up** - Create your driver account
2. **Upload Documents** - Submit required documents for verification
3. **Get Approved** - Wait for account approval (usually within 24-48 hours)
4. **Go Online** - Toggle online to receive delivery requests
5. **Accept Deliveries** - Accept requests that fit your schedule
6. **Complete Deliveries** - Follow step-by-step delivery instructions
7. **Get Paid** - Receive your earnings weekly or on-demand
