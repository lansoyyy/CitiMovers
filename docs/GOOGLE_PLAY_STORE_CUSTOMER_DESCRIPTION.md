## Full Description

**CitiMovers** is your trusted on-demand delivery service in the Philippines. Whether you need to send packages, move items, or transport goods, we've got you covered with professional drivers, real-time tracking, and secure payments.

### 🚚 Why Choose CitiMovers?

**Fast & Reliable Delivery**
- Book instantly and get matched with nearby professional drivers
- Real-time tracking from pickup to delivery
- Multiple vehicle options: Motorcycle, Sedan, Van, and Truck
- Estimated arrival times and fare transparency

**Easy & Convenient Booking**
- Simple pickup and dropoff location selection
- Save your favorite locations for quick booking
- Choose the right vehicle for your needs
- Get instant fare estimates before booking

**Real-Time Tracking**
- Track your delivery in real-time on the map
- Get step-by-step updates on delivery progress
- Know exactly when your package arrives
- Photo verification at key delivery stages

**Secure Payments**
- Multiple payment options via Dragonpay
- Built-in e-wallet for seamless transactions
- Easy top-up functionality
- Transparent pricing with no hidden fees

**Stay Connected**
- Chat directly with your driver
- Call your driver for quick coordination
- Real-time notifications for all updates
- 24/7 customer support

### 📱 Key Features

**Booking Management**
- Book deliveries in seconds
- View booking history
- Cancel bookings when needed
- Rebook with saved locations

**Vehicle Selection**
- 🏍️ Motorcycle - Small packages, quick delivery
- 🚗 Sedan - Medium-sized items, personal delivery
- 🚐 Van - Large items, furniture, appliances
- 🚛 Truck - Heavy cargo, bulk deliveries

**Delivery Tracking**
- Live map tracking
- Status updates: Pending → Accepted → In Progress → Completed
- Loading and unloading progress monitoring
- Photo confirmation at pickup and delivery

**Profile & Settings**
- Manage your personal information
- Add and edit saved locations
- Choose your preferred language (English, Tagalog, Cebuano, Hiligaynon, Ilocano, Waray, Bicolano, Pangasinan)
- Customize notification preferences
- Access help center and support

**Payment Options**
- E-wallet with easy top-up
- Dragonpay integration (credit/debit cards, online banking, over-the-counter)
- Secure payment processing
- Transaction history

### 🌟 How It Works

1. **Enter Locations** - Set your pickup and dropoff points
2. **Choose Vehicle** - Select the right vehicle for your delivery
3. **Confirm Booking** - Review fare and book instantly
4. **Track Delivery** - Watch your delivery in real-time
5. **Complete** - Receive confirmation when delivered
